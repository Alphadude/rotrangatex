General

Rotrangatex is a Nigerian company offering integrated engineering, procurement, construction (EPC), and marine services, particularly for the oil and gas industry. We also provide logistics, environmental, and manpower supply services.

Our head office is located at #48 Tombia Road, GRA Phase III, Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria.

We have been operating for over 10 years, steadily growing into a trusted service provider in Nigeria and the African sub-region.
